Shivaji Maharaj

Shivaji maharaj founder of the Maratha kingdom of India. The kingdom's security was based on religious toleration and on the functional integration of the Brahmans, Marathas, and Prabhus.

Sambaji Maharaj

was the second Chhatrapati of the Maratha Empire, ruling from 1681 to 1689. He was the eldest son of Shivaji, the founder of the Maratha Empire.
